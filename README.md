# learn-harmony-mockup1
First Markup of LearnHarmony for universities in Nigeria

Initial Mockup Scope: "The Compliance Pulse" (VC Desktop View)
To win over the VC, this initial mockup focuses on visual proof of compliance, turning a stressful multi-week manual process into a single-click export.
Screen Layout & Key Components
The Top-Line Metric Cards:
CCMAS Compliance Rating: A giant percentage score (e.g., 87% Audit Ready) that aggregates faculty activity across the university.
Active Digital Classrooms: Real-time fraction showing how many faculty members have logged an interactive session this week (e.g., 742 / 910 Faculty Members).
The Departmental Risk Matrix (Main Table):
A scannable breakdown of every department, highlighting where the VC needs to push Deans before the auditors arrive.
Faculty / Department | Active Adherence | CA Submissions | Audit Readiness StatusFaculty of Engineering | 94% | 100% Uploaded | Green (Ready)
Faculty of Science | 88% | 82% Uploaded | Yellow (Minor Gaps)
Faculty of Social Sciences | 51% | 40% Uploaded | Red (Action Required)
The Killer Feature Action Button:
Positioned prominently at the top right of the screen: [Generate NUC Audit Dossier (PDF)]. Clicking this instantly compiles and downloads the verified, timestamped course logs and continuous assessment metrics for the entire university, eliminating manual paperwork entirely.
